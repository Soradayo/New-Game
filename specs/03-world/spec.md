\# World Design



\## Overview



The setting is a fictional near-modern world.



Names are procedurally generated.



No real countries exist.



\## Requirements



World systems MUST include:



\- nations

\- organizations

\- social class

\- regions



\## Design



Nation archetypes:



\- imperial

\- commercial

\- religious

\- revolutionary

\- frontier



Organizations:



\- state

\- corporations

\- academia

\- religion

\- underground



Class:



\- lower

\- worker

\- middle

\- upper

\- special



Regions:



\- capital

\- industrial

\- academic

\- religious

\- frontier
